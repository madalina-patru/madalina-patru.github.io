Supervision

I am happy to co-supervise Bachelor's and Master's theses in the following areas:

Sustainable Finance & ESG: ESG performance and financial outcomes, green bond markets, climate risk in banking.
Banking & Systemic Risk: Banking regulation, macro-prudential policy, financial stability.
Distributional Effects of Policy: Property taxes and wealth, heterogeneous effects of monetary/fiscal policy.
