Thesis Supervision

I am happy to co-supervise Bachelor's and Master's theses in the following areas:

Sustainable Finance and ESG: ESG performance and financial outcomes, green bond markets, climate risk in banking.
Banking and Systemic Risk: Banking regulation, macro-prudential policy, financial stability.
Distributional Effects of Policy: Property taxes and wealth, heterogeneous effects of monetary and fiscal policy.

If you are interested, please send me an email with your transcript and a brief description of the topic you have in mind.
