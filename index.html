Madalina Patru

Doctoral Researcher in Economics · IWH Halle

Welcome! I am a Doctoral Researcher in the Department of Financial Markets at the Halle Institute for Economic Research (IWH) in Germany. My research focuses on sustainable finance, banking regulation, systemic risk, and the distributional effects of macroeconomic policies.

Research Interests

My work lies at the intersection of financial economics and public policy. I am particularly interested in sustainable finance, banking regulation, systemic risk, property taxes, and the distributional consequences of macroeconomic policies. My current projects explore the impact of property taxes on household wealth and how policy shocks transmit differently across income groups.
