Madalina Patru

PhD Student in Economics · IWH Halle

Welcome! I am a PhD student in the Department of Financial Markets at the Halle Institute for Economic Research (IWH) in Halle (Saale), Germany. My research focuses on sustainable finance, banking regulation, systemic risk, and the distributional effects of macroeconomic policies.

Research Interests

My work lies at the intersection of financial economics and public policy. I am particularly interested in sustainable finance, banking regulation, systemic risk, property taxes, and the distributional consequences of macroeconomic policies. My current projects study how changes in property taxation affect household wealth and how policy shocks transmit differently across income groups.
