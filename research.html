Research

My research is at the intersection of financial economics and public policy, with a focus on how macroeconomic and regulatory changes affect households and financial institutions. I am particularly interested in distributional consequences, sustainable finance, and systemic risk.

Work in Progress

Property Taxes and Household Wealth

This project studies how fiscal reforms in property taxation affect household wealth accumulation and homeownership decisions, with a focus on distributional outcomes across different household types.

Distributional Effects of Macroeconomic Policies

This project examines how monetary and fiscal policy measures affect households across the income distribution, studying the heterogeneous transmission channels of aggregate policy shocks.
